Reasoning Study

Instructions
For this experiment you will be shown a visualization and/or a text description describing a reasoning problem. At the bottom of the page will be a button labeled "I'm ready to see the question". Please take as much time as you want to understand the visualization and/or text. When you are ready to answer a few questions based on the information presented, please click the "I'm ready to see the question" button. The visualization and/or text will remain on the page with the questions.

If you are presented with a visualization, it may be static or interactive. If it is interactive, you will see a legend with checkboxes that you may click to check or uncheck as many times as you wish. Examples are shown below.
